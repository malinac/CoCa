1. Abstract

This document describes how 'Coca - COOL Calendar' works and how is encoded.

2. Introduction

Coca is a widget created by two studets from the Faculty of Computer Science . This would be the perfect planner for the user's time, perfect reminder of the birthdays and important eveniments where the user should be at a specific time.

Also the user can choose the way calendar will look for him by setting the color, font size and the dimention of it.
